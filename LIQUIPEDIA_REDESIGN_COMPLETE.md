## 🎯 Design Principles Applied

| Principle | Implementation |
|-----------|-----------------|
| **Information First** | Tables, clear hierarchies, readable content |
| **Professional Minimalist** | No unnecessary animations or effects |
| **Dark Theme** | `#0a0e27` background with high contrast text |
| **Clean Typography** | Inter font throughout, removed Orbitron |
